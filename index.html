The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh's journey from a war-torn, impoverished nation to a rising economic power in South Asia is remarkable and a testament to its people's resilience. The country has consistently demonstrated its ability to face and overcome challenges like natural disasters, political turmoil, or global financial downturns. Over the past few decades, Bangladesh has made significant strides in reducing poverty, improving healthcare and education, and boosting economic growth. However, after the July-August Revolution 2024, the next phase of its development, dubbed Bangladesh 2.0, seeks to build on these achievements by addressing existing challenges while capitalising on new opportunities."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
